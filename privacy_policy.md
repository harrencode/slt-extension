Privacy policy (template)

Short summary:
This extension reads data from your MySLT account pages inside your browser to compute usage summaries (total, peak, off-peak, extra, bonus, add-ons). All scraping and computation happen locally in your browser; no usage data is transmitted to external servers by this extension.

Details:
- What we collect: Usage values displayed on your MySLT pages (data volume, caps, used/remaining). These values are read only from pages you visit while logged into MySLT.
- Where data is stored: Data is stored locally in the browser using `chrome.storage.local` for the extension's operation.
- Why we collect it: To show your current usage and provide optional notifications about low add-ons or milestone usage.
- Data sharing: We do not share or send your data to any third-party servers.
- How to remove data: To clear stored data, open the extension settings in the browser and click "Remove" or uninstall the extension; storage will be cleared.
